Melodic-Death-Metal from Naila, Germany

After a long break, Endtime Prophecy is rocking since Septembre 2008. Inspired by bands like As I Lay Dying, Amon Amarth, Inevitable End oder Immortal Souls, our songs are Melodic Death with touches of Metalcore, Blackmetal, and Power Metal.

Members
